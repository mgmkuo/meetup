TrySomethingNew is a 3-week sprint project for Insight Data Science.
It uses collaborative filtering to recommend new Meetup activities. Traditional
recommendation systems present only the top scoring items, resulting in
suggestions that are just "more of the same." TrySomethingNew dives deeper
into the list to produce a diversity of recommendations.
